Tentacool (T3C0)

This model is perfect for residential applications. Space-saving design to fit in anywhere. The residential unit has a fast-charge mode in case of an overwhelming infestation near the end of the battery's charge. The Tentacool's six pest-fighting tentacles are as follows:

  • Tentacle one is an electro-zapper that targets large insects.
  • Tentacle two is an electro-zapper that targets small insects.
  • Wasps have no chance against tentacle three's flying insect immobilizing spray.
  • Tentacle four is dressed with a microfiber cloth for wiping away pest carcasses.
  • The fifth tentacle is outfitted with a squeegee to wipe away messes on smooth surfaces.
  • Tentacle six has disinfectant spray to ensure a germ free cleanup after the removal of pests.

Tentacruel (T3C3)

This model is perfect for commercial applications. It's housing is completely customizable. Any company-specific logo or design theme can be applied for a unique look. This model has everything from the Tentacool unit. Additionally, it has a multi-mode programmable schedule for day and night cycles. The Tentacruel models can be linked to one another through bluetooth so that multiple units can be used throughout a building. The Tentacrual has the six Tentacool tentacle and the following tentacles:

  • Tentacle seven is a uv sterilizer for high-impact cleanup.
  • Tentacle eight is a lazer rodent eliminator.
  • The ninth tentacle is a sonar detector to find hidden pests quickly.
  • A tenth tentacle features a mini vaccuum for waste collection.
  • The eleventh tentacle is a waste removal claw for large debris.
  • Tentacle twelve is the infrared remote targeting system for accurate pest elimination.
